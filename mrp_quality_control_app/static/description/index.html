Quality Control (QC) Management for Manufacturing

This app help to user for Quality Control like Quality Check and Quality Alert with Pass-Fail Type, Measure Type, Picture Type and Text Type for Manufacturing and Warehouse Operations. Also Generate Quality Check and Alert Report.Any company who manages manufacturing to produce different products, they need some MRP Quality Control and MRP inspection in Odoo, This app help to user for Quality Control like Quality Check and Quality Alert for Manufacturing so it coming easier to manage Manufacturing efficiently and reduce cost and waste of products. You can also Generate Quality Check and Alert Report in Odoo MRP with this Module.
